Hello! I am currently a master student at the Rapid-Rich Object Search (ROSE) Lab at Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Dr. Satoshi Tsutsui and Prof. Bihan Wen. Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Wuhan University. I have also worked as a research intern at Tsinghua University. I am dedicated to increasing the transparency and performance of modern models through interdisciplinary approaches.

My research interests primarily focus on computer vision. More specifically:

  • Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), particularly representation analysis
  • Medical Image Analysis
